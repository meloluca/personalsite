About Me

Hello! I'm Alex. My journey in tech started with a curiosity for how things work, and it's evolved into a career dedicated to building software that is not only functional but also intuitive and human-centered.

With over 5 years of experience in full-stack development, I specialize in React, Node.js, and cloud-native architectures. I thrive in collaborative environments where I can solve complex problems and learn from my peers.

When I'm not coding, you can find me hiking in the mountains, experimenting with new recipes, or diving into a good book. I believe in continuous growth, both professionally and personally.
